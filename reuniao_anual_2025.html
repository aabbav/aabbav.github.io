AABBAV Annual Meeting 2025

The annual AABBAV meeting aims to bring together Brazilian academics active in Belgium and Luxembourg, as well as other and other academics working with Brazilian partners or on Brazilian-related topics in these countries, for social and professional networking. The goal is to increase community awareness and integration, and to discuss issues relevant to this community, including scientific research, funding, social and professional integration, collaboration, outreach activities, and strengthening of academic ties between Brazil and Belgium.
